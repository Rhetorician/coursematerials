Please note that since Canvas will not render presentation notes, you need to submit two separate files:

    1. A PowerPoint document for your presentation
    2. A Word or PDF document containing just your script.

If you used Google instead of Word to make your presentation, follow these steps:

    1. On Google Slides, click on File > Download As > Microsoft PowerPoint (.pptx).
    2. On Google Slides, click on File > Print Settings > Preview. Here, choose the “1 slide with note” option and click the “Download PDF” button to export your speaker notes as PDF.

That's it!
