Reflection and Direction

Part 1: Pick any three career-related readings. For each, identify one meaningful idea and respond with substance. Don’t summarize. Don’t blindly agree. Extend the point, challenge it, or apply it to something real. Make an argument. (If none of the readings had value, come talk to me during office hours.)

Part 2: Reflect on how—or if—those ideas affect how you present yourself professionally. That includes your resume, cover letter, or interview strategy. How might something from a reading shape the way you frame your experience, voice your goals, or position yourself in the job market?

Grading Considerations: Strong submissions will be:

  • Reasoned
  • Genuine
  • No BS – no vague, generic, or empty claims.
